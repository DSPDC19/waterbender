What is water?

Water is a substance that is made up of two hydrogen atoms and one oxygen atom (H2O) and can be found in solid (ice), gas (steam) and liquid (water) states. The physical and chemical properties of water are very important for the survival of ecosystems.

Is water running out in the Dominican Republic?

According to data from the Latin American Observatory of Water and Sanitation Services (OLAS, 2023), 91.6% of Dominican households have access to water sources considered safe or improved. This access is higher in urban areas, at 94.2%, compared to 79.0% in rural areas.
